I am a senior researcher (permanent member) at Spatial Content Research Team, Hyper-Reality Metaverse Research Lab., Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI). I earned my Ph.D. in Electronics and Computer Engineering at Hanyang University under the supervision of Prof. Jong-Il Park. During my Ph.D., I was fortunate to join the computer vision group under the supervision of Dr. Slobodan Ilic in Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures & Augmented Reality (CAMP) at Technical University of Munich (TUM). Before joining the ETRI, I was a postdoctoral research fellow in Dept. of Virtual and Augmented Reality at Fraunhofer Institute for Computer Graphics (IGD) and collaborated with Dr. Harald Wuest.

Research Activity

My research interests are mainly 3D computer vision for augmented/mixed/extended reality, including 6D pose estimation/localization and mapping, low-level feature description and matching, and camera calibration. I am also interested in augmented/mixed/extended reality in industrial, medical, educational, art, or game applications.

To date, I have been collaborating with outstanding researchers: Prof. Kangsoo Kim (University of Calgary, Canada), Prof. Jong-Il Park (Hanyang University/Seerslab, South Korea), Dr. Jungsik Park (Seerslab, South Korea), Dr. Harald Wuest (Visometry GmbH/Fraunhofer IGD, Germany), Dr. Slobodan Ilic (Siemens AG Corporate Technology/Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany), Dr. Stefan Hinterstoissor (Intrinsic, Germany), Prof. Hideaki Uchiyama (NARA Institute of Science and Technology, Japan), Dr. Moon-Hyun Lee (LG Electronics, South Korea), Prof. Hanhoon Park (Pukyong National University, South Korea)
